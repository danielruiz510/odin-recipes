Classical Italian Lasagna

The Lasagna Dish Just Like Mama Use To Make

This lasagna dish brings back fond memories for me. Growing up my mother and grandmother made sure to make this atleast twice a month, usually for Sunday lunch. While there are many lasagna recipes out there this one is all done from scratch.

Everything from the sauce to the pasta sheets are done by hand. I know what you're thinking...Isn't this dish going to take to long? While the finished product will take you about an extra hour, I promise you it is well worth it.

Ingredients Needed For The Perfect Lasagna

  • Meat: This super meaty lasagna contain 3 types of meat. Ground beef, ground pork, and sweet and hot sausages can be found in the meat mix
  • Onion and Garlic: It wouldn't be italian cooking without these key ingredients.
  • Tomatoes: Make sure to only use big ripe tomatoes for this recipe, otherwise you might be a bit dissapointed.
  • Spices and seasonings: This lasagna recipe is flavored with a large amount of herbs and spices.
  • Pasta: Not just any type of pasta. This dish will contain only the finest lasagna noodles.
  • Cheeses: We are not shy of using cheese. This dish will contain 3 types of cheeses. Mozzarella, Parmesan, and Ricotta will make the perfect combination.

How To Make Lasagana Step-By-Step

Here's a very brief overview of what you can expect when you make homemade lasagna:

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. et the lasagna rest before serving.

How To Layer Lasagna

Make sure to follow the following order to achieve the perfect lasagna.

  1. Meat sauce
  2. Noodles
  3. Ricotta mixture
  4. Mozzarella slices
  5. Meat sauce
  6. Parmesan cheese
  7. Repeat the layers, then top with the remaining Parmesan.
